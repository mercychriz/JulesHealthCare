Our Comprehensive Services

Specialized care solutions tailored for seniors and children, delivered with compassion and expertise

Senior Care & Home Support

Registered Nurse (RN)

Provides medical care, administers medications, monitors health conditions, and coordinates patient care plans.

Personal Support Worker (PSW)

Assists seniors with daily living activities, personal hygiene, mobility support, and companionship.

Home Health Aide

Offers non-medical assistance, such as meal preparation, light housekeeping, and transportation.

Geriatric Care Specialist

Focuses on tailored senior care plans, ensuring emotional and physical well-being.

Physical Therapist

Aids seniors in improving mobility, strength, and independence through guided therapy.

Child Support & Development

Speech Therapist

Helps children develop communication skills, language comprehension, and articulation.

Occupational Therapist

Assists children in building fine motor skills, independence, and everyday functionality.

Child Development Specialist

Designs and implements programs to aid children in social, cognitive, and physical development.

Play Therapist

Uses structured play techniques to foster emotional growth and learning in children.

Feeding Specialist

Supports children with eating challenges, teaching healthy habits, and nutrition strategies.

Administrative & Support

Healthcare Coordinator

Manages patient care plans, schedules, and liaises between families and healthcare professionals.

Client Relations Manager

Ensures customer satisfaction, addresses concerns, and builds relationships with families.

Training & Development Officer

Oversees employee training programs for continuous skill enhancement.

HR & Recruitment Specialist

Hires, trains, and manages healthcare professionals to maintain service excellence.

Operations & Compliance Manager

Ensures adherence to healthcare regulations, safety protocols, and quality standards.

Emergency & Paramedical

Emergency Response Nurse

Provides immediate care in urgent situations for seniors and children.

Paramedic Support Staff

Assists in emergency medical interventions, ensuring prompt and effective care.

Palliative Care Specialist

Offers end-of-life support, ensuring comfort, dignity, and emotional care.
